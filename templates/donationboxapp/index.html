Donation

Donation is the act of giving goods, or services to a charitable organization or a person in need. It is a way to support causes that one cares about and make a positive impact in the world.Many organizations also accept donations of goods, such as clothing, toys, or shoes supplies. Donating is a personal choice.

Volunteering

Volunteering is a way to give back to one's community and make a positive impact on society. It also allows individuals to develop new skills, meet new people, and gain valuable experience. Volunteering is a personal choice, but it can be a rewarding and fulfilling experience that benefits both the individual and the organization they are volunteering with.

Awareness

Awareness for donation refers to the efforts to educate individuals about the importance and benefits of donating to charitable organizations or causes. It involves raising awareness about the needs of various communities and populations, and encouraging individuals to make a positive impact by donating their time, money, or resources.
